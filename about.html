About us

Here is all you need to know about us

Rainy Days was founded in Norway in 2011 and since then Rainy Days has grown to become a solid market leader in rain jackets. The group consists of around 2,000 committed employees who operate under the Rainy Days brands in Norway, Sweden, Denmark and Finland.

We at Rainy Days love everything to do with fashion and good quality, so we always make sure to be up to date on the season's latest trends. We will inspire you with news every season, so that you can easily find a look that suits you perfectly. We always want to provide our customers with exceptional customer service, great prices and fast delivery!
